WHAT I DO

KOREAN MAKEUP

Soft, youthful, and radiant. Focuses on dewy skin, gradient lips, and subtle eye makeup to enhance natural features. It's all about the “glass skin" look and a fresh, innocent vibe.

THAI MAKEUP

Glamorous and bold. Typically includes sharper brows, sculpted cheeks, and often bright lipstick. Thai makeup blends traditional beauty with modern glamour — great for events and photography.

DOUYIN MAKEUP

Trendy and digital-age inspired. Popularized on the Chinese TikTok app Douyin, this style features flawless porcelain skin, lifted eyeliner, sparkly eyes, and playful touches like faux freckles or blush under the eyes. Often described as “e-girl meets idol".

NATURAL MAKEUP

Minimal and clean. The goal is to look effortlessly polished — think neutral tones, light foundation, soft mascara, and barely-there lip color. Perfect for everyday wear and enhancing your natural beauty.

BRIDAL MAKEUP

Elegant, timeless, and camera-ready. Often customized for each bride, but typically includes long-lasting foundation, soft contouring, romantic eyeshadow, and a radiant finish. Designed to look flawless in person and in photos.
